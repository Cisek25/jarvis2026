About the apartment

A cosy retreat with quick pool access

Eagle Nest is 85 m² designed with comfort in mind. Located on the 2nd floor of Eagle Tower — with easy access to the pool area and a private terrace overlooking the Mediterranean Sea and lush greenery.

  • Private terrace with sea views and golf course greenery
  • Air conditioning in all rooms
  • Master bedroom with en-suite bathroom and terrace access
  • Flexible bed configuration — twin or king 160×200 cm
  • Sofa bed in the living room — extra sleeping space for guests
  • Washer and dryer in a separate utility room
  • Fast Wi-Fi — ideal for remote work
  • Two full bathrooms — no queues at full occupancy
