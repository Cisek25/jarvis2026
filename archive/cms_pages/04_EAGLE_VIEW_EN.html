About the apartment

A view you'll never forget

Eagle View is an apartment for those who want to see Benidorm from above. From the 14th floor you get an unbroken panorama of the sea, the Sierra Helada mountain range and the city skyline. Three independent bedrooms and a large terrace create a comfortable space for families and groups alike.
