The building

Quality you'll feel from the moment you arrive

Eagle Tower is a modern residential building in the Poniente district — with an elegant lobby, full CCTV coverage and silent lifts. The gated, green grounds border directly on Las Rejas golf course, creating a setting far removed from the tourist bustle of the centre.

  • Elegant lobby with reception and entrance area
  • 24/7 CCTV throughout the building
  • Silent lifts — no noise or vibration
  • Gated, fenced grounds with access control
  • Underground parking — space next to the apartment
  • Adjacent to Las Rejas golf course (Pitch&Putt, 9 holes)
Water zone

Water all year round

Main Pool

Large outdoor pool with sun loungers and relaxation area. Sunny exposure for most of the day — ideal for unwinding after a walk around Benidorm or a day at the beach.

Jacuzzi

Heated jacuzzi next to the main pool. A water massage after an active day — especially pleasant in the evenings when the water temperature contrasts with the cooler air.

Children's Zone

A separate, shallow pool for younger guests with a safe non-slip surface. Parents can relax nearby while keeping their children within sight.

Sport zone

Active without leaving the building

Padel Court

Full-size padel court within the building. One of Spain's most popular sports — a great opportunity to try it for the first time or train regularly.

Multi-Sport Pitch

Sports surface suitable for basketball, volleyball and other team games. Good lighting allows play even after dusk.

Gym with a View

Fully equipped gym with cardio machines and free weights. Panoramic windows overlooking the sea and mountains — a workout with a view that motivates better than any poster.

Comfort & security

Peace and convenience around the clock

Private Underground Parking

Dedicated parking space next to the apartment in the underground garage. Access by card or remote — no searching for street parking, no parking fees, no stress. Ideal for guests travelling by car.

Gated Grounds & CCTV

Fenced grounds with access control and a 24/7 CCTV system. Cameras cover entrances, parking areas and common spaces. A peaceful holiday without unnecessary security concerns.
