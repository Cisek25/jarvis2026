What's nearby

Key points of interest

Poniente Beach Forget fighting for a spot on the sand. Poniente is the wider, calmer and better-maintained sister of Levante beach. Golden sand, crystal-clear water and a beautiful promenade perfect for evening strolls or jogging. 15 min walk / 3 min by car
Golf on your doorstep For golf fans, Eagle Tower's location is a dream come true. The Las Rejas Open Club Benidorm is right next door — a 9-hole Pitch & Putt course, ideal for beginners and advanced players looking to practise their short game. Right next door
Dining & shopping Mercadona and Aldi supermarkets just a few minutes' drive away, perfect for grocery runs. The Poniente promenade offers great restaurants — from fresh seafood to international cuisine. 5–10 min walk
Alicante-Elche Airport Just 40 minutes via the AP-7 motorway. Quick and easy access with no need to navigate narrow city-centre streets. Direct flights from most major European cities. 40 min via AP-7
Public transport Bus stops nearby connecting the district with the city centre and shopping malls. Easy connections to Levante beach and neighbouring Costa Blanca towns. Within walking distance
How to get here

Getting to Eagle Tower

By plane

Alicante-Elche Airport (ALC) — 40 minutes via AP-7. Direct flights from most major European cities. Transfer by taxi or hire car.

By car

AP-7 motorway (Autopista del Mediterráneo) — exit Benidorm Poniente. Navigation: Calle Presidente Adolfo Suárez 14. Private underground parking included in the apartment price.

By bus

ALSA — direct connections from Alicante bus station. From the airport: L11 bus or taxi to Alicante city centre, then regional bus to Benidorm.
