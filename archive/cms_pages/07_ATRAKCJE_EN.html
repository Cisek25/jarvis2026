Base camp

Benidorm — more than you think

Eagle Tower is in one of the best-connected locations on the Costa Blanca. Within 15 minutes by car you'll find four theme parks, two water parks, a historic old town, a panoramic viewpoint overlooking the sea and dozens of restaurants. This is not just an apartment by the beach — it's a base camp for the whole region.

Theme parks

Four parks, one neighbourhood

Terra Mítica One of Spain's largest theme parks — divided into zones inspired by the ancient civilisations of Egypt, Greece, Rome, Iberia and the Mediterranean Islands. Rides, shows and themed restaurants. Perfect for a full day out with families and groups. 5 min by car
Aqualandia One of Europe's largest water parks, with the continent's tallest water slide — Megaloda. Dozens of slides, a lazy river, pools and children's areas. A full day of adrenaline and fun. 10–15 min by car
Aqua Natura A water park combined with animal enclosures — a rare combination of pool relaxation and nature observation. Sea lions, parrots, reptiles and exotic birds in a green setting. A good choice for families with younger children. 10–15 min by car
Terra Natura A new-generation zoo — animals live in environments recreating natural habitats, without bars or glass. A walk among free-roaming animals. Rhinos, giraffes, tigers and hundreds of bird species. 5 min by car
Nature & culture

The other face of Benidorm

Balcón del Mediterráneo & Old Town A seafront viewpoint with a spectacular panorama of both beaches and the skyscraper skyline. Right next door — the historic old town with narrow streets, the Santa Ana church and tavernas serving tapas and paella. An evening stroll is a must. 10 min by car
Benidorm Island — Isla de Benidorm A small island opposite Levante beach, a nature reserve. Excursion boats run from Benidorm harbour several times a day. On the island: snorkelling, underwater wildlife and crystal-clear water. Boat from harbour — 30 min
Shopping & markets

Shopping centre & local markets

La Marina / Finestrat Centre Modern shopping centre with over 100 stores — Zara, H&M, Mango, Primark and other brands. Multi-screen cinema, restaurants and a food court. Air conditioning and comfort in the middle of the Spanish summer. 5 min by car
Mercadillos Markets Traditional street markets in the Rincón de Loix district — Wednesdays and Sundays. Fresh fruit and vegetables, souvenirs, clothing, jewellery. A colourful, local atmosphere and the chance to taste regional specialities straight from the vendor. 10 min by car — Wed & Sun
