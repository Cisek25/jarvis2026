About the building

Eagle Tower – Your Private Resort in the Heart of Benidorm

Eagle Tower is one of the most distinctive landmarks on the map of modern Benidorm. This impressive high-rise stands proudly in the Poniente district, offering a new definition of coastal living on the Costa Blanca. Its slender white silhouette and glass terrace balustrades were designed to make the most of natural light and deliver the spectacular views this region is famous for.

Water zone

Water All Year Round

The Costa Blanca sun is best enjoyed by the water. Our guests have exclusive access to an extensive pool area that satisfies both adults and the youngest visitors alike.

Main Pool

A spacious pool with crystal-clear water, perfect for swimming and cooling off on hot days. Surrounded by a sun-lounger area where you can sunbathe in peace — sunny exposure for the greater part of the day.

Jacuzzi

In need of a moment to recharge? The heated jacuzzi beside the main pool lets you unwind in the bubbles while gazing up at the blue sky. Especially enjoyable in the evenings, when the warmth of the water contrasts with the cooler night air.

Kids' Zone

A dedicated, shallower pool area for the youngest guests, featuring a safe anti-slip floor. Parents can relax nearby while keeping their children in sight — ideal water fun for the whole family.

Sports zone

Sports & Recreation at Your Fingertips

For those who cannot imagine a holiday without keeping active, Eagle Tower offers professional sports facilities included in the price of your stay.

Padel Court

Try Spain's most popular sport! A full-size, glass-enclosed padel court on the premises — the perfect opportunity for a friendly match or regular training sessions.

Multi-purpose Sports Court

A court with a sports surface suitable for basketball, volleyball and football. Good lighting allows play after dark — feel like a game? The court is at your disposal.

Gym with a View

Don't give up your training routine. Modern cardio equipment and free weights, plus glass walls framing a panoramic view of the sea and mountains — workouts here become a pleasure, not a chore.
