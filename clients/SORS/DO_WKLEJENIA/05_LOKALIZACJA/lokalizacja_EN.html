Poniente & Las Rejas

The best address in Benidorm

Benidorm has two faces. By choosing Eagle Tower, you opt for the more exclusive, peaceful and green one. Our building is located in the Poniente district, right next to the Las Rejas golf course. A location that guarantees the quiet you need for work or relaxation, while keeping all the resort’s attractions within a short walk.

approx. 15 min walk

Poniente Beach

Forget the fight for towel space. Poniente Beach is the wider, calmer and better-maintained sister of Levante Beach. Golden sand, crystal-clear water and a beautiful promenade perfect for evening strolls or jogging.

This is where Benidorm locals come to unwind.

Right next door

Golf on your doorstep

For golf enthusiasts, Eagle Tower’s location is a dream come true. The Las Rejas Open Club Benidorm is right next door. It’s a 9-hole Pitch & Putt course, ideal for beginners and advanced players alike who want to practise their short game under the shade of pine trees.

Neighbourhood

Dining & shopping

Everything you need for comfortable living is right around the corner:

  • Supermarkets (Mercadona, Aldi) — just a few minutes’ drive, perfect for a bigger grocery run.
  • Restaurants along the Poniente promenade — from fresh seafood to international cuisine, far from the typical fast-food tourist bars.
Getting here

Transport

  • Alicante-Elche Airport — just 40 minutes via the AP-7 motorway. A quick, easy drive with no need to navigate narrow city-centre streets.
  • Public transport — nearby bus stops connect the district to the city centre and shopping malls.
