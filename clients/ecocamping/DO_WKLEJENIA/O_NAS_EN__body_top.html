Our Story

A Glamping Experience Like No Other

Eco Camping Spytkowice brings a new standard of outdoor hospitality to southern Poland. We were born from a passion for nature and the belief that camping doesn't have to mean compromise.

Our property spans over 7 hectares of greenery, surrounded by forest and the rolling fields of the Carp Valley. 50 spacious safari tents offer full comfort — from quality beds and air conditioning to private terraces with a swing.

It's the perfect base for visiting Energylandia (5 min by car), but also a place you won't want to leave — with a swimming pool, outdoor cinema, dining area, and playground for kids.
