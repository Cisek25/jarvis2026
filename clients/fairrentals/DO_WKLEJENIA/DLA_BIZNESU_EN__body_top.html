Why an apartment, not a hotel

Home comfort, hotel flexibility

Staff on assignment need more than a bed. A full kitchenette, a washing machine, fast Wi-Fi — in our apartments these are the standard, not an upgrade.

For stays longer than a week, the comfort difference is felt from day one. The financial difference is felt in the invoice: a monthly rate often comes in below a mid-range hotel, with incomparably better quality of life.

What you get

The package for business clients

Six things that distinguish corporate stays with us. All transparent — pricing visible from the start.

VAT invoice

Full VAT invoice issued to the company after every stay. Net payment terms available once a framework agreement is in place.

Monthly rates

For stays above 28 days we provide a custom quote with a meaningful discount on the daily rate. Fixed price for the whole rental period.

Dedicated account manager

One phone number and one email address for every booking. No internal hand-offs, no waiting for someone else to reply.

Self check-in

Door code sent on arrival day. Your colleague can check in at any hour — after a night flight or a late train.

In-stay housekeeping

For longer business stays we provide regular cleaning with hotel-grade linen and towel turnover — included, no extra charge. Your team comes back from the office to a fresh apartment.

Portfolio scale

21 apartments across several Wrocław districts — we can almost always find the location that fits your assignment, even during fair weeks.
