Why an apartment, not a hotel

Home comfort, hotel flexibility

Staff on assignment need more than a bed. A full kitchenette, a workspace with a second screen, a washing machine, fast Wi-Fi — in our apartments these are the standard, not an upgrade.

For stays longer than a week, the comfort difference is felt from day one. The financial difference is felt in the invoice: a monthly rate often comes in below a mid-range hotel, with incomparably better quality of life.

Full VAT invoice issued to the company after every stay. Net payment terms available once a framework agreement is in place.
