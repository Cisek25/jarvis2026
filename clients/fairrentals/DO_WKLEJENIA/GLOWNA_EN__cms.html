About us

A family business from Wrocław

Fair Rentals is family-run. We manage every apartment ourselves — from the first key handover to the last cleaning. We know that details matter most, and we look after them personally: fast communication, uncompromising cleanliness, and the flexibility to match changing guest plans.

Personal touch

Each booking is a relationship for us, not a transaction. We answer messages after hours, advise on the right apartment for your stay, and handle any urgent situation immediately.

Proactive care

We don’t wait for issues — we anticipate them. Each apartment is checked before arrival, guests are looked after during the stay, and owners receive ongoing reports on the condition of their property.

Verified quality

Our average score on Booking is 9.8 and on Google 4.7. The result of hundreds of stays and a single, consistent standard — whether the guest stays for one night or one month.
