Frequently asked questions

FAQ for owners

Five questions we hear most often from owners considering handing their apartment to us.

What if I want to withdraw the apartment from rental?

The agreement includes a 30-day notice period with no penalty. After it expires we return the keys, hand over a closing report and a list of open bookings to honour — or we can transfer them to another apartment in the portfolio.

How is revenue split?

Standard split is 70/30 (Owner/Fair Rentals) on net revenue after platform commissions. For properties with parking, sauna or premium location we negotiate a better share. All operational costs (cleaning, consumables) are itemised transparently in the monthly report.

Who covers repairs and equipment costs?

Minor repairs up to PLN 200 we handle without consultation (deducted from your share with receipt). Larger items (appliance damage, painting, furniture replacement) we report with a contractor quote — decision is yours. Standard consumables (paper, cleaning supplies, coffee, tea) are on our budget.

Can I keep the apartment for personal use?

Yes. Block dates in the calendar — for your stay, family members, friends. No limits, no surcharges, no need to explain. Standard cleaning after your stay included in the service.

What about my apartment’s security?

Each guest is verified before booking (Booking ID or ID card). Electronic locks with individual per-stay codes — no physical keys in circulation. Liability insurance for damage beyond normal wear. Quarterly inventory for each apartment — photo report of equipment condition.
