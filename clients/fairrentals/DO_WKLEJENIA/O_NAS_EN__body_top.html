How it started

From one apartment to twenty-one

Fair Rentals began with a single apartment in Wrocław in 2018. Agnieszka — with management experience and a passion for hospitality — decided to apply hotel-grade standards to the short-term rental sector. She knew one thing: if you treat a Guest like a guest, they come back.

The company grew over time. More apartments joined, new processes, tools, integrations — all so that quality could be repeatable, not accidental.

Today we manage 21 apartments in Wrocław. Every one received in person. Every one with a consistent standard. A 9.6 average on Booking and 4.7 on Google isn't a target — it's the consequence of how we treat each booking and each Owner.
