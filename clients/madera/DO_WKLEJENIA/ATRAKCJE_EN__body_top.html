Prime location

Silesia within reach

Madera apartments are set in a quiet area near Silesian Stadium — one of Poland's most iconic sports venues. Silesian Park, one of the largest urban parks in Europe, is just a 10-minute walk away. Katowice city centre with the legendary Spodek arena, NOSPR concert hall and the Silesian Museum is reachable by public transport in minutes. A bus stop just 250 m from the entrance provides a direct connection to Katowice-Pyrzowice Airport.

On foot or a short walk

Right on your doorstep

Silesian Park One of the largest urban parks in Europe — over 600 hectares of greenery, lakes and walking trails. Perfect for a morning run, cycling or a relaxed afternoon in the shade. Directly adjacent to the apartments.
10 min walk
Silesian Stadium An iconic 54,000-seat stadium — Poland's largest sports arena. National team matches, major concerts and mass events. Visible from the apartment windows.
15 min walk
Legendia Silesian Amusement Park The largest amusement park in Silesia, set within Silesian Park. Roller coasters, rides for children and teens, seasonal events. A great choice for a full family day out.
10 min walk
Silesian Planetarium One of the oldest and largest planetariums in Poland, in operation since 1955. Screenings in the spherical cinema, astronomy exhibitions and an observatory. A remarkable experience for children and adults alike.
10 min walk
By public transport

Katowice City Centre

Spodek An iconic multi-purpose arena — one of the most recognisable architectural landmarks in Poland. World-class concerts, trade fairs, sports galas and e-sports tournaments. Spodek is the beating cultural heart of Katowice.
10 min
NOSPR — National Polish Radio Symphony Orchestra One of the most modern concert halls in Europe. Outstanding acoustics and a rich programme spanning classical to contemporary. Tickets are worth booking well in advance.
12 min
Silesian Museum Spectacular architecture — a museum built on the grounds of a former coal mine. Permanent exhibitions on Silesian art, history and ethnography. A modern multi-level display, partially underground.
12 min
Katowice City Centre — Mariacka Street & Nikiszowiec Mariacka is Katowice's main promenade, lined with restaurants, cafés and nightlife. Nikiszowiec is a unique early 20th-century miners' district, listed as a Polish Historic Monument — not to be missed.
15 min
From your base

Day trips

Auschwitz-Birkenau Memorial and Museum The former Nazi concentration and extermination camp — one of the most important memorial sites in the world, inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List. A deeply moving historical experience. Online ticket reservation well in advance is mandatory.
approx. 1 h drive
Energylandia Poland's largest amusement and water park — over 70 attractions including some of Europe's biggest roller coasters. Separate themed zones, an aquapark and an area for young children. A full day of thrills for the whole family.
approx. 1.5 h drive
